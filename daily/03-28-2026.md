- Rooted in the principle of Least Privilege
- goal is to create a "Default Deny" posture
	- only certain traffic is permitted, and everything else is discarded.
- Default Deny Stance (Implicit Deny)
	- Start with assumption that no traffic should be allowed to pass
	- important because it prevents backdoor access through forgotten ports and services
- Rule order is important is well because they are processed from top to bottom
	- Rules should also be ordered most specific to least specific
